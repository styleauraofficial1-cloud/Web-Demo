IMAGE PROMPTS AND SPECS FOR PORTFOLIO DEMOS

Use these prompts to generate or collect better images for the salon, dental/clinic, and restaurant/cafe portfolio pages.

Best overall format:
- Hero images: 16:9 landscape, 1920x1080 minimum, ideally 2400x1350 or 3000x1688
- Section/card images: 4:3 or 3:2 landscape, 1600x1200 or 1800x1200
- Detail/close-up images: 1:1 square, 1200x1200 or 1600x1600
- Portrait/team images: 4:5 portrait, 1600x2000
- File type: JPG or PNG
- Quality: sharp, natural lighting, no blur, no watermark
- Avoid: fake text, unreadable signs, distorted hands/faces, heavy filters, logos unless it is the real business logo
- Naming style: salon-hero.jpg, salon-service-1.jpg, clinic-hero.jpg, cafe-food-1.jpg

Recommended image count per demo:
- Minimum: 5 images
- Good: 8 images
- Best: 10-12 images

PHOTO SELECTION CHECKLIST
==================================================

Before sending me images, check:
- Is the subject clear within 2 seconds?
- Is it sharp on mobile?
- Is there enough empty space for headline text if used as a hero?
- Does it look like a real local business, not a fake stock ad?
- Are faces/hands natural?
- Is there no random text or watermark?
- Does the color mood match the business type?

Best hero photo rule:
Pick the image that makes the business feel real, trustworthy, and worth contacting.
