FIVERR IMAGE PACKAGE AUDIT AND STRUCTURE

Audit date:
2026-06-06

Overall verdict:
The final Fiverr gig images are strong and usable. They look premium, consistent, and much more professional than generic marketplace thumbnails. The package now has 5 specific folders, one for each Fiverr gig, with exactly 5 images per gig.

Main strengths:
- Each gig has 1 main image and 4 side/gallery images.
- Visual style is consistent across all gigs.
- Colors feel premium: warm white, deep teal, charcoal, and gold accents.
- Main headings are large and readable.
- Images use strong mockups: laptop, phone, dashboards, forms, workflows.
- The 1618x972 / similar sizing is close to Fiverr's recommended 1280x769 ratio.
- The layouts look high-ticket and agency-style, not cheap or random.
- Each gig tells a clear story across the 5 images.

What to watch before uploading:
- Some small text inside UI mockups may not be readable on mobile. This is acceptable if the main headline is readable.
- Fiverr thumbnails should rely on the big headline, not tiny details.
- If Fiverr crops any image, adjust by adding more empty space around the edges.
- Avoid uploading the extras folder as final gig images unless you intentionally choose one as a replacement.

Recommended upload order for each gig:
1. main image
2. side-1 image
3. side-2 image
4. side-3 image
5. side-4 image
